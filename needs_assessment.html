Resources for Quaternary Science Education: Needs Assessment
Neotoma's Outreach and Community Engagement team is working on organizing existing resources for Quaternary science education and creating new resources to fill gaps. In order to do so in a way that best serves Quaternary science educators, we are seeking input from those educators on what the most important concepts and methods of Quaternary science are, and where the greatest gaps in the Quaternary education toolset lie.
